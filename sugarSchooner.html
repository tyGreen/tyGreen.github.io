About Us

At Sugar Schooner, we do not believe in sacrificing flavour for presentation. We specialize in professionally presented breads and pastries with that nostalgic homemade flavour.

When it comes to our baking, we also do not believe in shortcuts! As the sun rises over Schooner Pond Shore every morning, so too do our bakers. This is not for the breathtaking view! Rather, it is to ensure everything we bake is of the freshest possible quality for you, our customers.
